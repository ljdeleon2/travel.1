About the Corozal Project

For my final project, I have chosen to create a website about Corozal, Puerto Rico. Corozal, located in the central-eastern region, is less known compared to popular destinations like San Juan or Ponce. I chose Corozal because my wife’s family is from there, and she highly recommends visiting. The project aims to highlight the town’s culture, history, and charm, presenting it as a unique travel destination. Thank you for visiting the website and exploring Corozal with me.
